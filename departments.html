What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

Christian Hospital Sialkot offers a full range of specialised medical and surgical departments, staffed by experienced clinicians dedicated to delivering quality care to every patient.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Our Obs & Gynae department is the cornerstone of CHT's mission. We provide comprehensive women's health services from adolescence through menopause, with particular focus on safe motherhood and reducing maternal mortality in the region.

Antenatal Care Safe Delivery Postnatal Care C-Section Family Planning Gynae Surgery High-Risk Pregnancy Infertility Counselling

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department is dedicated to the health and well-being of children from newborns to adolescents. We provide preventive, diagnostic, and therapeutic services in a child-friendly environment staffed by caring professionals.

Newborn Care (NICU) Vaccination Growth Monitoring Malnutrition Treatment Acute Illness Pediatric Surgery Respiratory Care Developmental Assessment

General Surgery

Our surgical team performs a comprehensive range of elective and emergency procedures. The department is equipped with modern operating theatres and a fully trained anaesthesia team ensuring patient safety and the best possible surgical outcomes.

Abdominal Surgery Appendectomy Hernia Repair Cholecystectomy Thyroid Surgery Emergency Surgery Laparotomy Wound Care

General Medicine

Our Internal Medicine department diagnoses and treats a wide variety of acute and chronic medical conditions. Our physicians take a holistic approach to patient care, addressing the root causes of illness and supporting long-term health management.

Diabetes Management Hypertension Cardiac Care Respiratory Diseases Infectious Diseases Gastroenterology Neurology Chronic Disease Management

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics department provides expert care for all conditions affecting the bones, joints, muscles, and musculoskeletal system. We treat injuries, degenerative conditions, and congenital disorders, restoring mobility and improving quality of life.

Fracture Management Joint Disorders Spine Care Sports Injuries Bone Infections Trauma Surgery Cast & Splinting Physiotherapy

Ophthalmology

Our Eye Department provides comprehensive vision care to patients of all ages. CHT has a long history of serving the high burden of preventable blindness in the region, with regular cataract surgical camps and community outreach programmes.

Cataract Surgery Glaucoma Treatment Refractive Errors Diabetic Eye Disease Ocular Trauma Eye Infections Vision Testing Spectacle Prescription

Emergency & Trauma

Our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We are equipped to handle all medical and surgical emergencies with speed and expertise. Our trauma unit manages road traffic accidents, burns, and other critical injuries.

24/7 Emergency Care Trauma Management Burns Treatment RTA Management Resuscitation Critical Care Triage Poison Management

Laboratory & Radiology

Our diagnostic services provide the foundation for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment across all departments. The laboratory and radiology facilities are equipped to handle a comprehensive range of diagnostic tests and imaging studies.

Haematology Biochemistry Microbiology Histopathology X-Ray Ultrasound ECG COVID Testing
