Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment for OPD?

Walk-in patients are welcome during OPD hours (Mon–Fri 8:00 AM–1:00 PM, Sat 8:00 AM–12:00 PM). For specialist consultations, we recommend calling ahead to check availability and reduce waiting time.

Is emergency care available at night?

Yes. Our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including nights, weekends, and public holidays. A duty doctor is always on call to attend to emergencies.

What if I cannot afford to pay for treatment?

Christian Hospital Sialkot is committed to serving all patients regardless of ability to pay. Subsidised and free care is available for those in genuine financial need. Please speak with our reception staff or social welfare officer about available support options.

Do you offer maternity and delivery services?

Yes. Maternity and safe delivery services are central to our mission. Our Obstetrics & Gynecology department provides antenatal care, skilled birth attendance, postnatal care, and emergency obstetric services around the clock.
