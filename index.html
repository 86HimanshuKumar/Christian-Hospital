Welcome to CHS

A Legacy of Faith & Healing

Christian Hospital Sialkot (CHS) has been a beacon of hope and healing in the Punjab province for over 100 years. Founded on the principles of Christian service and humanitarian care, we have stood steadfast through generations — providing compassionate, quality healthcare to those who need it most.

"In loving God, and for His glory, CHS is committed to provide accessible quality healthcare to women, children and other underserved people."

Our dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and support staff work tirelessly to ensure every patient receives the attention, dignity, and care they deserve — regardless of their ability to pay.

Why Choose Us

Why Patients Trust Us

For over 100 years, patients have trusted Christian Hospital Sialkot for our unwavering commitment to excellence, compassion, and faith-driven care.

Experienced Staff

Our team of over 60 dedicated physicians, surgeons, and specialists bring decades of combined expertise across all medical fields. Continuing education ensures our staff stay at the forefront of modern medicine.

Modern Equipment

We invest continually in state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic equipment to provide accurate diagnoses and effective treatments — bringing advanced medical technology to the people of Sialkot and Punjab.

Compassionate Care

Every patient who walks through our doors is treated with dignity, respect, and genuine care. Our Christian values guide us to serve the whole person — body, mind, and spirit — in every interaction.
