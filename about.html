Our Story

A Legacy of 100+ Years of Service

Christian Hospital Sialkot (CHS) was established around 1924 by Christian missionaries who were moved by the enormous medical needs of the people of Sialkot and the surrounding tribal regions of what is now Punjab, Pakistan.

What began as a small dispensary offering basic medical aid has grown, over more than a century and a half, into a fully equipped hospital providing a wide range of specialist medical and surgical services. Throughout wars, natural disasters, disease outbreaks, and social upheaval, CHT has remained open and committed to its founding mission.

The hospital has served successive generations of families from Tank and neighbouring districts, earning deep trust and affection among communities who might otherwise have no access to quality healthcare. Many patients travel hours — sometimes from across the region to receive treatment at CHS.

Today, CHT continues to be operated as a non-profit Christian institution, receiving support from local communities, national organisations, and international partners who share our vision of healthcare as a fundamental human right.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

In loving God, and for His glory, CHS is committed to provide accessible, quality healthcare to women, children, and other underserved people. We serve all — regardless of faith, ethnicity, or ability to pay — with the compassion and dignity that every human being deserves.

Our Vision

Our vision is a healthier, stronger community in Sialkot and the wider Punjab region — a community where no woman dies in childbirth from preventable causes, where every child has access to vaccinations and treatment, and where the most vulnerable people receive the same standard of care as anyone else.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These four pillars guide everything we do — from how we treat our patients to how we train our staff and invest in our facilities.

Faith

Our work is rooted in the Christian belief that every person is made in the image of God and deserves to be treated with love, dignity, and respect. Faith motivates and sustains everything we do.

Compassion

We approach every patient, family member, and colleague with empathy and genuine care. We see beyond diagnoses to the whole person — their fears, their hopes, and their humanity.

Excellence

We are committed to continuous improvement in clinical quality, patient safety, and service delivery. Our staff regularly undergo training to maintain and elevate the standard of care we provide.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability — in our finances, our clinical decisions, and our relationships with patients, staff, donors, and the community we serve.

Our History

Key Milestones

Over 155 years of growth, resilience, and unwavering commitment to the communities we serve.

1924

Hospital Founded in Sialkot

Christian missionaries establish a small dispensary in Tank to serve the local population and surrounding tribal communities, beginning a legacy of faith-based healthcare.

Early 1900s

Expansion of Services

The dispensary grows into a full hospital with inpatient wards, an operating theatre, and maternity services — responding to the region's growing population and healthcare needs.

1947

Transition at Independence

At Pakistan's independence, CHT continues its mission uninterrupted, serving communities through a period of major regional upheaval with compassion and dedication.

1970s – 1990s

Modernisation & Growth

Major infrastructure upgrades including new wards, laboratory facilities, and specialist clinics. The hospital expands its bed capacity to serve over 200 patients simultaneously.

2000s

Specialist Services Launched

Dedicated departments for Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, and Emergency & Trauma are established, bringing specialist care that was previously unavailable in the Sialkot district.

2020 – 2022

COVID-19 Pandemic Response

CHS serves as a frontline facility during the COVID-19 pandemic, providing testing, treatment, and community vaccination services while maintaining all essential services.

2024

New Infrastructure & Facilities

Completion of a new boys hostel and the establishment of private patient rooms, further enhancing the hospital's capacity and comfort for patients and medical students.

2025

100+ Years of Service

Christian Hospital Sialkot celebrates over 100 years of continuous service — a testament to the enduring power of faith, compassion, and dedication to community health.
